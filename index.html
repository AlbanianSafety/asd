FEASIBILITY STUDY

The service consists in the analysis and in the analytical evaluation of the existing company characteristics, calculating the costs and the possible results of a project on the basis of a preliminary idea.

The feasibility study consists of an illustrative report containing:

a) the functional, technical, management, economic-financial characteristics of the works to be carried out;

b) the analysis of the possible alternatives with respect to the identified implementation solution;

c) verification of the possibility of realization through the public private partnership;

d) the analysis of the state of affairs, in its possible architectural, geological, socio-economic, administrative components;

e) the description, for the purposes of the prior assessment of the environmental sustainability and the landscape compatibility of the intervention, of the requirements of the project to be designed, of the characteristics and connections with the context in which the intervention fits, with particular reference to the verification environmental, historical, archaeological, landscape restrictions that interfere with the areas or buildings affected by the intervention, as well as the identification of appropriate measures to safeguard environmental protection and cultural and landscape values.

SURVEY OF WORKING ENVIRONMENTS Finalized for the preparation of the Risk Assessment Document (DVR), the Single Document for the Assessment of Interference Risks (DUVRI) and the Business Emergency Plan.

ELABORATING THE DOCUMENT DIVALUTATION OF RISKS
With the evaluation of all risk including specific risks such as:
Chemical Agents;
Noise;
Vibration;
Ionizing Radiation and Radon;
Electromagnetic fields;
Artificial Optical Radiation;
Microclimate;
Asbestos;
Biological agents;
Cancerogens.

The Risk Assessment also includes;
1. Identification of PPE (Personal Protective Equipment) for the performance of duties, formation, information and training;
2. Assessment of risks specific to the tasks of the types of workers;
3. The Training, Information and Training Program;
4. Evaluation of specific risks specific to the tasks of the types of workers.

DRAFTING OF THE SAFETY OPERATING INSTRUCTIONS (IOS).

DRAFTING OF SAFETY MANAGEMENT PROCEDURES (PGS).

MODULE NECESSARY TO THE MANAGEMENT OF SAFETY IN THE WORKPLACE

WORKER INFORMATION FILE

DRAFTING THE BUSINESS EMERGENCY PLAN
With the evacuation plan and fire fighting plans, rules of conduct in case of emergency.

PROGRAMMING OF THE EVACUATION TESTS AND PROGRAMMING

PROGRAMMING AND CONVOCATION OF THE PERIODIC MEETING
For Companies with more than 15 employees, with relative drafting of the Periodic Meeting report.

DRAFTING OF THE DUVRI
Single Document for Interference Risks for Health and Safety of Workers.

MANAGEMENT SURVEY FOR SAFETY
Inspection every six months to check if the conditions of the workplace and the duties have remained unchanged or not.

DRAFT UP UPDATES
If there are substantial changes detected during the management inspection:
- DVR update;
- Updating of individual risks: chemical, noise, biological agents, vibrations, ionizing radiation and radon, etc.;
- DUVRI update;
- Updating of the Company Emergency Plan and / or the evacuation plan and fire prevention plans;
- Assistance and advice in case of prescriptions of the competent control bodies;

Environmental measurements and monitoring.
Wich: - Monitoring of the concentration of radon gas in underground workplaces;
- Phonometric measurement of noise exposure levels;
- Measurement of vibration exposure levels of the hand-arm system (HAV) and the whole body(WVB);
- Measurement of the intensity of electromagnetic fields and related exposure levels;
- Measurement of the emission intensity of Artificial Optical radiation;
- Mapping and echoing materials containing asbestos and related levels of exposure.
